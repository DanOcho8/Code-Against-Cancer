Frequently Asked Questions

CodeAgainstCancer is a platform dedicated to providing resources, support, and community for individuals affected by cancer. Our goal is to empower users with tools to manage their health journey, find recipes, connect with support groups, and access valuable information.

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button located in the top-right corner of the homepage. You’ll be guided through a simple registration process, where you can provide your details and create a profile.

Once you are logged in, navigate to the "Forum" section from the sidebar. Here, you can browse threads, participate in discussions, and share your experiences with others.

Visit the "Recipes" page from the main navigation bar to explore a collection of healthy and cancer-friendly recipes. You can search for recipes based on your dietary needs and preferences.

To update your profile, go to the "Manage Profile" section from the sidebar. Here, you can update your personal information, including your type of cancer, date of diagnosis, and other relevant details.

The "Resources" section provides a variety of helpful articles, links to support groups near you, and tools to manage your health. These resources are curated to assist you in every step of your journey.

If you need additional assistance, you can reach out to us via the "Contact Us" page. Our support team is available to help you with any questions or issues you may have.
